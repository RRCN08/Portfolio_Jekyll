ONESTOP IITG

Time-table Addon for the ultimate college companion.

OneStop IITG is an application that allows IIT Guwahati students to lodge complaints, get notified about events in campus, and a get lot more done, quickly.

I was tasked with adding a time-table feature to the existing app, keeping in mind the design language of the same.

For: Students' Web Committee, IIT Guwahati

Duration: Oct 2019 to Jan 2020

Status: Completed

Link: Google Play Store

OVERVIEW

OneStop was born out of the need for a unified app amongst students for their everyday life on campus at IIT Guwahati. First released in February 2019, the app replaced old and obsolete Hello IITG app.

Over one year, the app has gained over 5000+ installs, but one of the major features it lacked was a functioning time-table for the students.

CHALLENGES

Breakdown of complex time-tables of multiple disciplines.
Following the design language of the current app.

DESIGN PROCESS

RESEARCH

There are 2 undergraduate programs and 5 post-graduate ones. Apart from this, there are 10 different branches of Engineering. The first year UG students share a common time-table. After spending 2 days deciphering all of the different time-tables, I broke it into 2 different types, Engineering and Design.

Task flow for Engineering Time-Table Entry

Task flow for Design Time-Table Entry

Engineering branches are divided into multiple sections, making timings within the branch itself different. They were identified using their roll numbers and lab group. Design time-tables had a different story altogether. Their classes are divided into modules. Each week is one module. Every course is assigned to a set number of modules. On top of this, first-year design students have engineering courses as well. Coupled with their core courses, students have to do HSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) courses in their 4th, 5th, 7th and 8th semesters.

Complicated enough.
