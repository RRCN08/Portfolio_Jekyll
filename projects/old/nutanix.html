Nutanix Summer Internship

Monitoring the entire IT ecosystem in a large-scale enterprise.

Nutanix provides a leading next-generation enterprise cloud platform that converges traditional silos of server, virtualization and storage into one integrated solution that can also connect to public cloud services.

During my 2 months over there, I worked on their flagship product, Prism, which is an end-to-end management solution for IT administrators to centrally manage their entire hardware stack including storage, compute and virtual machines (VMs) all from a single tab.

For: Nutanix Inc.

Duration: May 2021 to July 2021

Status: Completed

Link: Not Available

OVERVIEW

I worked with the Prism Operations team under which I worked on the Analysis section.

I was tasked with redesigning the section to give IT administrators to gain visibility to their entire IT ecosystem beyond just infrastructure. What started out as a redesign of one of the pages ballooned into a birth of a completely new page, thereby the product to a certain extent.

DESIGN PROCESS

The project included several rounds of brainstorming sessions with my mentor, along with intense research into the problem space. It was supported by competitor analysis of tools in the monitoring space on a feature basis, and further on the user experience as well. Paper sketches of concepts and medium-fidelity wireframe iterations were created and was further discussed with the developers to see as to whether the proposed ideas aligns with where the product is headed.

During the final weeks, I began converging onto the finer details and incorporating the Nutanix Design guidelines. The final set of deliverables included high fidelity mock-ups for Prism and a comprehensive report of the competitor analysis.

LEARNINGS

This was my first time working with an enterprise company, and the opportunity to spearhead my own project from start to finish was refreshing.

I got to also co-create with other designers and the developers from the team had a design-friendly approach. Coupled with handling the industry deadlines and working together with people from different time-zones, the internship proved to be an invaluable learning experience.
