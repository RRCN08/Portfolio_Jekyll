LINK MAGAZINE

Technical Board Magazine for IIT Guwahati.

This year, for their 7th edition, the TechBoard of IIT-G introduced their first ever magazine, showcasing all the events, projects, and competitions their team has participated in.

We got the opportunity to design the magazine from front to end, keeping in mind the spirit of The Technical Board, to evoke inspiration amongst students to engage more with the technical community.
